Private residence in South West London. The design
features a contemporary style with flat fronted
doors, matt black bespoke ironmongery, and
book matched marble splashback.
The floorboards cabinetry that blends in with the
Dinesen flooring, creating a contrasting rustic
feel that adds warmth to the space.
