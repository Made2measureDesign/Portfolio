Once a middle-class neighborhood. Bushwick
is now a popular hipster enclave in deep
Brooklyn, combining a thriving art, and bar
scene with an older, more entrenched Hispanic
culture.

This 14 story hotel, will accommodate an art
gallery, two skate parks (in the rooftop and
third level) 8 stories of rooms and a restaurant.
