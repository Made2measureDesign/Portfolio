Private residence in South West London. The project
consisted of the refurbishment of the kitchen and dining
area. The cabinetry has a traditional shaker paneling
with stainless steel finish on the ir onmongery.
The space presents a classical British but with contemporary
details as exposed bricks and herringbone
oak flooring.
